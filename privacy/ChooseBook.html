ChooseBook Privacy Policy

Hyperana

ChooseBook uses the internet to fetch additional content from its own web pages. It does not solicit information from the user or show ads or solicitations from any third party. The app does not collect, store, or sell personal information except what is generated automatically by Google Play Services for purposes of analytics and troubleshooting through the developer account.

  • What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

    The Application does not collect usage statistics or other user data except the following:
    1. Data automatically generated for any application installed and used on an Android device through Google Play Services.
    2. Information the user may provide through the application's Feedback menu.

  • User Provided Information

    Through the Google Play Store's beta tester program and through the feedback link within the Application, the user is invited to provide feedback about the Application through email. If a user chooses to email the developer, the information in that email, including "From" address, will be collected. The developer may use this information to contact the user for the purpose of clarifying or troubleshooting issues or notifying of changes or updates to the product.

  • We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information

    1. as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process
    2. when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request
